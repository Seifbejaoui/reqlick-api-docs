## Features

Here are some of the features that Reqlick offers:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Advanced Analytics" icon="chart-line">
    Reqlick provides powerful analytics for your links, including geolocation,
    device, browser, and referrer information.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Branded Short Links" icon="link">
    Reqlick offers free custom domains on all plans for you to create branded
    links that stand out.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Unlimited QR Codes" icon="qrcode">
    QR codes and short links are like peas in a pod. Reqlick offers free and
    Unlimited QR codes for every short link you create.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="File To link" icon="files">
    Transform all your files (PDFs, PowerPoint, images, Excel, GIFs, and more)
    into trackable branded short links within seconds.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Personalization" icon="user">
    Customize your link's behavior with device targeting, link expiration, link
    utm, and more.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Team Collaboration" icon="users">
    Invite your teammates to collaborate on your links, QR codes and files.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
